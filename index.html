About

Small studio,
deliberate work.

Sidus is built around the belief that good software comes from caring about the problem more than the output. Every product here starts small — with a clear use case, real users, and a genuine reason to exist.

Based in the Netherlands. Registered at the KvK. Building in public, one app at a time.

Thoughtful by default Every decision — from feature scope to visual detail — gets considered. Nothing ships that isn't ready.
User first, always Products are built around genuine needs. No dark patterns, no bloat, no unnecessary complexity.
Independent & sustainable Sidus is self-funded and intentionally small. Growth follows quality, not the other way around.
